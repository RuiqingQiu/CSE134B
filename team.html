Developers for Team XYZ

Team XYZ is a group of UC San Diego web developers looking to make people's lives better through health-related apps. The team's current project and goal is to make the best food-diary web app possible so people can start on their journey to reach their health goals. Using source control, validated HTML, the best industry practices, the finest taste in web design, coupled with a lean, move fast, fail fast, recover fast culture, XYZ will set the world's calories on fire.
